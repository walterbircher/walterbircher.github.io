Privacy Statement

cureEDMD respects your privacy and is committed to transparent data practices.

What information we collect

When you visit our website, we may collect limited information including your IP address and how you interact with our pages. If you donate, volunteer, or join our registry, we collect contact information and details relevant to your involvement.

How we use your information

We use information to:

  • Provide services and support you have requested
  • Improve our website and programs
  • Send updates and communications (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at info@cureEDMD.org to exercise these rights.

Third parties

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted partners who assist with our mission, all under confidentiality agreements.

Contact us

For questions about this privacy statement or our data practices, email info@cureEDMD.org.
