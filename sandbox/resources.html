FAQ

Why registries are important

What is a patient registry?

A registry collects health information over time to help researchers understand disease patterns.

Why should I register?

In rare diseases, better data helps improve care guidance and trial readiness.

Can caregivers register on behalf of a child?

Yes, many registries allow parents or guardians to complete forms for pediatric patients.
