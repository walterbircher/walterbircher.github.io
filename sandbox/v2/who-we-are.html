Who We Are

cureEDMD was established in 2025. Before the foundation was formally launched, the founders had already been raising and directing funding for EDMD research for many years.

Mission

We connect patients, families, clinicians, researchers, and drug developers so effective treatments can move faster.

What we are solving

EDMD is a genetic condition that can cause serious muscle weakness, joint stiffness, and dangerous heart complications.

Why this matters

Families need better options now, and long-term research support to reach safer and more effective treatment pathways.
