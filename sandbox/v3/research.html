Research

Based on cureEDMD source pages, current research focus is in two main areas: treatment-target discovery and gene therapy.

Priority 1: Find useful treatment targets

Identify pathways in muscle and heart cells that might be treated with medicine. This includes checking whether existing FDA-approved medicines could help EDMD.

Priority 2: Advance gene therapy

Develop gene-based approaches for EDMD forms linked to EMD and LMNA, with realistic steps toward future clinical translation.

Why patient data matters

Natural history and registry data help define trial-ready outcomes and make studies stronger.
